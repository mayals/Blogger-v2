About Codes City Blog, What is this blog for ?







G reetings and welcome to my blog! Codes City Blog , my name is May, and I come from an engineering and electronics background. Ever since I started using the internet, I have been captivated by the world of web development and its intricacies.

The idea of simply being a user who browses websites and applications without understanding how they were created felt like a form of ignorance to me. I ended up considering questions like, "How are these applications fabricated?", "What processes do these pages go through before reaching us?" and "What are the foundational concepts behind them?".

These inquiries just extended my advantage and drove me to examine any article or book that spoke about the subject. However, I soon discovered that the world of web development is complex and requires in-depth knowledge of various concepts and foundations. I realized that if I wanted my curiosity to be satisfied, I would need to commit to learning web development seriously and embark on the journey of creating web applications myself.

My love for learning web development grew, and I became particularly passionate about working with the backend framework, Django.

As I progressed in my knowledge, I found myself curious about how to consume the APIs I produced using Django Rest Framework. . This led me to learn the frontend framework, React , which helped me showcase my work on the frontend framework.

Now, on Codes City Blog, I am excited to share the experiences and knowledge I have gained during my web development journey with you. I aim to be a source of knowledge, someone who contributes to the web as much as they absorb from it. I hope to assist anyone who, like me, loves coding and is seeking a starting point. Feel free to explore the site and begin your own journey of web development.

Happy Coding!
